Howdy, I'm Emma!

It’s so nice to meet you! Thanks for coming by to learn a little more about me. I’m Emma and I’m currently enrolled in the full-stack coding bootcamp at UT Austin. I chose to embark on this journey for three simple reasons:

  • Career switch that was fulfilling
  • Learn patience with myself
  • Become a part of a community

I was born & raised in El Paso, TX. Growing up I swam competitively from the age of 3(!) all the way through my junior year of college where I retired at the ripe old age of 22. While earning my Bachelors degree in Crimonology at University of Texas Permain Basin, I decided I wanted to pursue law school. I began the strenuous process of preparing for the LSAT, taking it, getting internships, letters of recommendation I needed – all while finishing my undergrad. Well, somewhere along the way I realized that law wasn’t the right fit for me. That brought me to this bootcamp and I can confidently say this was the BEST decision I could have made.

I’ve had jobs all across the board, from swim coach to IT tech to waitress…My most valuable assets are my work ethic, adaptiveness, and attention to detail. Ok, so that last one can also be a detriment but only sometimes!

Below are a few pictures of my boyfriend Ben, our cat Laulu, and of course myself! We live in Austin, TX and love exploring the city. Going to concerts is a personal favorite or sitting outside (when it isn’t raining!) reading a good book (preferably a thriller) soaking up that sunshine.
